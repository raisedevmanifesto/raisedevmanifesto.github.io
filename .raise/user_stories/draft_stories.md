# Draft User Stories

These stories are under consideration for future iterations of the RAISE Manifesto site. They are not yet committed to the current implementation.

---

## Cosign the RAISE Manifesto

**As a** supporter of the RAISE Manifesto,

**I want to** add my name to a public list of cosigners,

**So that** I can show my support and signal alignment with its principles.

### Acceptance Criteria
- There is a clear way for visitors to express support (via email, form, or automated system).
- Cosigners’ names (and optional messages/links) are displayed on the site.
- The process is simple, transparent, and respects privacy/consent.
- The feature can be added in a future iteration without disrupting the current simplicity.
