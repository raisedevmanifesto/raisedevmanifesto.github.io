RAISE Manifesto Website Vision

Purpose Statement

To create a definitive resource that clearly articulates the RAISE methodology while simultaneously demonstrating its principles through its own implementation.

Problem

The rise of AI assistants in software development brings unprecedented opportunities for acceleration and innovation, but without a clear methodology, it often leads to:

  • Fragmented, incoherent software artifacts
  • Loss of developer agency and intention
  • Technical decisions driven by ease of implementation rather than purpose
  • Documentation that quickly becomes stale and disconnected from code

Solution

The RAISE Manifesto website will:

  1. Clearly articulate the RAISE methodology's principles, artifacts, and practices
  2. Provide a living example of intention-driven development
  3. Showcase real-world implementations of RAISE across different project types
  4. Demonstrate how visually focused articulations of intention lead to higher-quality software
  5. Establish a foundation for a community of practitioners

Core Audience

The website is designed primarily for:

  • Software Engineers working with AI assistants who want a structured methodology
  • Technical Leaders seeking to maintain quality and intentionality in AI-assisted teams
  • Non-Technical Stakeholders who want to participate meaningfully in the development process
  • Educators and Students exploring the evolution of software engineering practices

Success Metrics

We will measure success through:

  • Clarity and completeness of the methodology description
  • Site accessibility across devices and assistive technologies
  • Number and quality of example projects demonstrating RAISE
  • Community engagement and adoption of the methodology
  • Alignment between the site's artifacts and implementation

Non-Goals

To maintain focus, this project explicitly excludes:

  • Creating a complex content management system
  • Building interactive tools or generators
  • Implementing a forum or discussion platform
  • Serving as a comprehensive resource for all AI development practices

This vision document was created at the project inception and guides all subsequent development decisions. It is revisited regularly to ensure alignment.
