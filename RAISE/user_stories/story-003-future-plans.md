# Story 003: Outline Future Plans

**As a** reader of the RAISE Manifesto,

**I want to** learn about the future plans related to the RAISE methodology,

**So that** I can see where it's headed and understand its potential evolution.

---

## Acceptance Criteria
- The site includes a clearly identifiable section for "What's Next" (future plans).
- This section outlines future plans, including the development of applications and tools using RAISE, as detailed in the design document (e.g., short intro sentence, 2-4 bullet points).
- The presentation of this section is concise and clear.
- The section is located on the main page, after the "Who We Are" section and before the discussion links, accessible via scrolling.
