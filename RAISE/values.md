# Core Values

These values guide the creation, stewardship, and evolution of the RAISE Manifesto and its site:

- **Simplicity**: Strive for clarity and ease in both design and communication. Complexity is only added when it serves genuine purpose.
- **Transparency**: Make decisions, processes, and changes visible and understandable to all. Openness builds trust and invites collaboration.
- **Humility**: Recognize that no methodology or artifact is perfect. Remain open to feedback, revision, and learning from others at every stage.
- **Bravery**: Have the courage to question assumptions, invite critique, and pursue bold ideas—even when they challenge the status quo.
- **Accessability**: Designed and developed to meet high accessibility standards, aiming for compliance with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, and aspiring to Level AAA where feasible. Ensuring a "world-class" experience for screen reader users is a primary objective.
