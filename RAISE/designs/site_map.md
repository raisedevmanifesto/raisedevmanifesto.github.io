*   **`/` (Homepage)**
    *   **Manifesto Content:** The main text of the RAISE Manifesto.
        *   *Potential internal anchors for long sections, e.g., `#section1`, `#section2`.*
    *   **"Who We Are" Section:** (Scroll-to section, e.g., conceptual anchor `#about`)
    *   **"What's Next" Section:** (Scroll-to section, e.g., conceptual anchor `#next`)
    *   **Discussion Links Section:** (Scroll-to section, e.g., conceptual anchor `#discuss`) - Located at the very end of the page.
